Welcome to PIMA Guitars!

Hello fellow music enthusiasts! PIMA Guitars is your premier destination for quality guitars and top-notch service right here in Albay. Our journey began in 2020 when the lack of accessible, high-quality guitars in the region inspired us to transform a computer shop in Taysan into a haven for guitar lovers.

PIMA, derived from Spanish, represents the thumb (Pulgar), index (Indice), middle (Medio), and ring (Anular) fingers used in guitar playing. These fingers symbolize our commitment to providing top-notch service, repairs, and products.

Since our opening on July 19, 2020, we've been dedicated to offering a curated collection of guitars and accessories that cater to your musical aspirations. At PIMA Guitars, it's not just about selling instruments; it's about enhancing your musical journey with quality and passion.

Step into PIMA Guitars, where your musical harmony begins. We're excited to be part of your musical story. Welcome to the PIMA family!
